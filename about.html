Meetings

Open to the public.

Meetings take place on the second Thursday of each month. Thd doors open at 6:30pm and the meeting begins at 7:00pm. For every meeting an SWMHS club member will give a reptile themed presentation. Afterwards members will stay and discuss plans for the future and socialize.

Anyone can become a member. Membership dues are $15 per person or $20 for the entire family. Dues go towards funding SWMHS events or field trips. Events and field trips are open to payed members only.
